Ecology of Chief Logan State Park 2

A Further Study of the Ecology of Chief Logan State Park, Logan County, West Virginia Field Survey Conducted on 5-6,7,8,9-08 by Dr. Robert Hunsucker (see also our 8-27-31-07 Survey) With a Dedication to the Opponents of Mountain Top Removal / Valley Fill Mining
also see Chief Logan 1

Introduction

    These inventories complement those of Aug. 2007, particularly with respect to the fauna, and birds in particular.
