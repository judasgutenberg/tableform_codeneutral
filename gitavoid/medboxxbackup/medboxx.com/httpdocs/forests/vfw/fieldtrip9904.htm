Introduction to Appalachian Biodiversity

Two day intensive field trip sponsored by Virginians for Wilderness
Led by Dr. Robert Hunsucker and Dr. R.F. Mueller
For Professionals and Serious Amateurs

Location : We will explore the diverse Blue Ridge Mountain flora between 1600 and 4000 feet asl in the Mt. Pleasant area.Emphasis will be on gaining practical knowledge of the Appalachian flora, including medicinal and edible plants and fungi. Correlation will also be made between certain species and climate, rock and soil types on which they occur. Date of Trip Saturday May 29 and Sunday May 30, 1999
